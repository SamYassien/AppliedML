x_test /= 255.0

The fashion mnist image size is 28 x 28. When flattened we will have 784 predictors. These are too many parameters and would slow down our training for little gain to accuracy performance. To fix that, we apply dimentionality reduction to the dataset using principle component analysis (PCA) to reduce the parameters the 100 most important predictors to our classification problem.

def plot_conf_matrix(matrix, labels):
    plt.imshow(matrix, interpolation='nearest', cmap=plt.cm.autumn_r)
    plt.title("Confusion Matrix")
    plt.colorbar()
    tick_marks = np.arange(len(labels))
    plt.xticks(tick_marks, labels, rotation=90)
    plt.yticks(tick_marks, labels)
    plt.tight_layout()
    plt.ylabel('True label')
    plt.xlabel('Predicted label')
    width, height = matrix.shape
    for x in range(width):
        for y in range(height):
            plt.annotate(str(matrix[x][y]), xy=(y, x), 
            horizontalalignment='center',
            verticalalignment='center')
